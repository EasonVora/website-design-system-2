Web Design Color Schemes

Color schemes in web design set the tone, improve usability, and shape user perception. Monochromatic, analogous, and neutral schemes create a harmonious or professional look for corporate, lifestyle, and luxury brands, while complementary and triadic schemes offer vibrant contrast ideal for e-commerce, educational, and creative sites. Dark mode, split-complementary, and double-complementary schemes add modern, balanced contrast, making them popular on tech, gaming, and content-rich sites.
